== Screenshots ==
1. Example of a Wikipedia link. Activate this campaign and connect your site to Wikipedia
2. Preview of an impression of a inText link campaign. You can optionally choose an icon which is placed after the link.
3. Place a widget when a certain keyword is in the text. Widgets can be iframes or simple image and text combinations.
4. Campaign dashboard with recommended campaigns. Get live tracking of impressions, clicks and click through rate
